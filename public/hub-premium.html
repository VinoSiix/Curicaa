Common questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this a subscription?
Yes — but with no long-term commitment. Choose monthly or annual billing. You can cancel anytime, and you'll keep access through the end of your billing period. No hidden fees, no contracts.
What happens if I cancel?
You keep full access through the end of your current billing period. No partial charges, no penalties. Your progress is saved in case you want to come back later.
What if I have multiple children in different grades?
The Full K–12 Bundle is perfect for families with multiple kids. You get access to all 8 grade levels, so each child can work at their own pace.
Can I switch from monthly to annual?
Absolutely. You can upgrade to annual billing at any time from your account settings. We'll prorate the difference so you never overpay.
Do I need to be a teacher to use this?
Not at all. Every lesson includes step-by-step parent guides, warm-up activities, and assessment tips. If you can read, you can teach with this.
What materials do I need?
Most activities use common household items like paper, pencils, measuring cups, and kitchen supplies. We specifically design for low-budget, accessible learning.
Is there a money-back guarantee?
Yes. We offer a full 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied for any reason, just let us know and we'll refund your purchase.

Our story

Built by a parent,
for every family.

When I started homeschooling my own kids, I couldn't find a curriculum that was complete, affordable, and actually easy to use. Everything was either $1,000+ per child, required hours of prep, or came with expensive lab kits I'd never use.

So I built Curicaa — open-and-go lesson plans from age 5 through SAT prep, using materials you already have at home. Every lesson is laid out week by week so you can focus on teaching, not planning.

If you can read, you can teach with this. That was the goal from day one, and it still is.
