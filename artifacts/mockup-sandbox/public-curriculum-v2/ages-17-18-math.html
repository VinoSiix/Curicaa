Digital SAT — Math Section
The Digital SAT Math section (70 min, 44 questions) covers algebra (~35%), advanced math (~35%), problem solving & data analysis (~15%), and geometry/trigonometry (~15%). A built-in Desmos calculator is available for all questions.
