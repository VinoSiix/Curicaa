Key TakeawaysBull vs. bear and the sweet spot

Bull Case

  • 3.4M+ homeschool students growing 5%/year
  • 81% of families have 2+ kids = family pricing wins
  • $169/yr replaces $600-$1,800 in traditional curriculum
  • 100K users + 3.5% conversion = $591K/year
  • 500K users (achievable) = $2.96M/year
  • LTV:CAC of 3.5:1 = sustainable growth

Bear Case

  • EdTech has the lowest freemium conversion of any SaaS (2.6%)
  • 50-70% annual churn typical
  • Free alternatives (Khan Academy, Easy Peasy) are formidable
  • Competing against free is hard
  • Homeschool parents are budget-conscious

The Cheat Sheet

Revenue = Users x Conversion x $169
  • At 3.5% conversion, every 1,000 free users = ~$5,915/year
  • Every 10,000 free users = ~$59,150/year
  • Every 100 free users = ~35 paying customers
  • Each paying customer = ~$423 LTV
