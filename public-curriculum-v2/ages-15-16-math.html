GED Test — Mathematical Reasoning
The GED Math test (115 min) covers two sections: quantitative problem solving (~45%) and algebraic problem solving (~55%). A TI-30XS calculator is allowed on most of the test. Passing score: 145.

GED Preparation · Ages 15–16

Build mastery in number sense, algebra, geometry, and statistics — the four content domains of the GED Mathematical Reasoning test — through concept instruction, practice problems, and timed exam simulation.
