30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We're confident that Curicaa will transform your homeschooling experience. That's why every paid subscription comes with a 30-day money-back guarantee.

If the curriculum doesn't meet your family's needs within the first 30 days of your purchase, we'll refund your payment in full — no questions asked.

Refund Eligibility

To be eligible for a full refund:

  • The refund request must be made within 30 calendar days of your original purchase date
  • Your account must be in good standing (not suspended for Terms of Service violations)
  • The refund applies to your first purchase of a given plan (renewals are handled differently — see below)

Plan-Specific Details

  • Single Grade ($79/yr or $9/mo): Full refund within 30 days of initial purchase
  • Full K-12 Bundle ($169/yr or $19/mo): Full refund within 30 days of initial purchase
  • Curicaa Pro ($259/yr or $29/mo): Full refund within 30 days of initial purchase
  • Free Preview: No payment — nothing to refund

Monthly Subscriptions

For monthly plans, a refund is available within 30 days of your first payment. Subsequent monthly payments are non-refundable but can be cancelled at any time (see Cancellation below).

How to Request a Refund

Requesting a refund is simple:

  1. Email us at support@curicaa.com with the subject line "Refund Request"
  2. Include your account email address and the reason for the refund (optional, but helpful)
  3. We'll confirm receipt within 1 business day and process your refund

There are no forms to fill out, no phone calls to make, and no hoops to jump through.

Processing & Timeline

  • Refund initiation: Within 2 business days of receiving your request
  • Payment processor: 3–5 business days for credit card refunds
  • Your bank: Up to 5 additional business days to appear on your statement

Total estimated time: 5–10 business days from your refund request to seeing the funds back in your account.

Refunds are issued to the original payment method used for the purchase.

Subscription Cancellation

How Cancellation Works

You may cancel your subscription at any time, for any reason:

  • From your account: Settings → Subscription → Cancel Subscription
  • Via email: Contact support@curicaa.com with "Cancel Subscription" in the subject

What Happens When You Cancel

  • You keep full access to all paid content until the end of your current billing period
  • No further charges will be applied
  • Your account reverts to the Free Preview plan at the end of the billing period
  • Your progress and preferences are retained in case you resubscribe

Annual vs. Monthly

  • Annual plans: Cancellation takes effect at the end of your annual billing period. No partial refunds for unused months after the 30-day guarantee period.
  • Monthly plans: Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current monthly billing cycle. No charge for the next month.

Reactivation

Cancelled subscriptions can be reactivated at any time. Your progress and settings will be restored. If you cancelled within the 30-day guarantee window and received a refund, you may re-subscribe at the current listed price.

Exceptions & Notes

  • No partial refunds are issued for unused time after the 30-day guarantee period on annual plans
  • Duplicate charges will be refunded in full upon verification — contact us immediately
  • Technical issues that prevent access to the Service for an extended period may qualify for a credit or refund at our discretion
  • Price adjustments: If you purchased within 14 days of a price reduction, contact us for a credit of the difference

We handle every refund request with respect and without judgment. Our goal is to make this process as painless as possible — we want you to be happy, and if Curicaa isn't the right fit, we understand.
