Sound Waves: Pitch and Volume

Age 9 Science · November Week 2

Age 9

Waves & Information Transfer
🎯 Learning Goal

Investigate how the speed of vibrations (frequency) affects pitch and how the size of vibrations (amplitude) affects volume.

☀️ Warm-Up

Hum a low note, then a high note. Place your hand on your throat — can you feel the vibrations change speed?

🔬 Activity: Pitch and Volume Experiments

Test each experiment and record what you discover:

✅ Can Your Child...?
  • Explain that faster vibrations create higher pitch
  • Demonstrate that larger vibrations create louder sound
  • Distinguish between pitch and volume
  • Show the ruler experiment and explain the result
💡 Easier: Focus on loud vs. quiet (amplitude) only.
💡 Harder: Human ears can hear roughly 20 to 20,000 vibrations per second (Hertz).
