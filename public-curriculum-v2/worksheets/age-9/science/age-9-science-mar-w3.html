Animal Adaptations for Survival

Age 9 Science · March Week 3

Age 9

Animal Structures & Senses
🎯 Learning Goal

Identify physical and behavioral adaptations that help animals survive, including camouflage, mimicry, hibernation, and migration.

☀️ Warm-Up

Show a photo of a chameleon changing color, an arctic fox in white winter fur, and a monarch butterfly. Why do these animals look the way they do?
