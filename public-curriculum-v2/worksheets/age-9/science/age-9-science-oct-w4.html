Building an Electromagnet

Age 9 Science · October Week 4

Age 9

Electricity & Magnetism
🎯 Learning Goal

Construct an electromagnet by wrapping wire around a nail and connecting it to a battery, and investigate factors that affect its strength.

☀️ Warm-Up

Show a regular magnet sticking to the fridge. What if we could turn a magnet on and off whenever we wanted? That's an electromagnet!
