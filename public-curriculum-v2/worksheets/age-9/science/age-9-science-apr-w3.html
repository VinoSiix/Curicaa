Building Prototypes with Household Materials

Age 9 Science · April Week 3

Age 9

Engineering Design Process
🎯 Learning Goal

Transform a design plan into a physical prototype using common household and recycled materials, practicing basic construction skills.

☀️ Warm-Up

Review your blueprint from last week. A prototype is a first version — it does not need to be perfect, it needs to be testable!
