Electricity & Magnetism
🎯 Learning Goal

Build a complete series circuit using a battery, wire, and a small light bulb, and understand that electricity needs a closed loop to flow.

☀️ Warm-Up

Look at a flashlight. Turn it on and off. What's happening inside to make the light turn on? Open it and examine the batteries and the metal contact strip.

✅ Can Your Child...?
  • Build a working circuit independently
  • Explain that breaking the loop stops electricity flow
  • Draw a circuit diagram with battery and bulb symbols
  • Use the words "open circuit" and "closed circuit" correctly
💡 Easier: Use a battery holder with pre-attached wires and snap connectors.
💡 Harder: Build a parallel circuit with two bulbs — ask what happens when you unscrew one bulb.
