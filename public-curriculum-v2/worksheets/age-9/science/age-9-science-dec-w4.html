Landforms and Topographic Maps

Age 9 Science · December Week 4

Age 9

Earth's Surface Features
🎯 Learning Goal

Identify common landforms (mountains, valleys, plains, plateaus, canyons) and understand how topographic maps represent 3D elevation on a 2D surface.

☀️ Warm-Up

Show photos of a mountain, a valley, a canyon, and a flat plain. Name each landform and discuss what force might have shaped it.
