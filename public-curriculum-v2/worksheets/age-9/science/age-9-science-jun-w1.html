Science Fair & Year-End Review
🎯 Learning Goal

Select a testable scientific question, form a hypothesis, and design an experiment with controlled variables using the scientific method.

☀️ Warm-Up

Look back through the year. Which experiment was the most fun? Which topic made you the most curious? That curiosity is the starting point for a science fair project!
