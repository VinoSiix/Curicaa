Age 9 Science · January Week 4

Age 9

🎯 Learning Goal

Distinguish between weather (short-term atmospheric conditions) and climate (long-term patterns), identify cloud types, and practice basic weather forecasting.

☀️ Warm-Up

Is it usually hot in July where we live? That is climate. Was it hot yesterday specifically? That is weather. Discuss the difference.
