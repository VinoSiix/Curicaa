🎯 Learning Goal

Explain the process of photosynthesis — plants use sunlight, water, and carbon dioxide to make sugar (food) and release oxygen.

☀️ Warm-Up

Take a deep breath in. You just breathed in oxygen that plants made! Breathe out — you just gave plants carbon dioxide. It is a partnership.
