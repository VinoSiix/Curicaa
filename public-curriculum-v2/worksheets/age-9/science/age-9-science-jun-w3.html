Creating Display Boards and Presenting Findings

Age 9 Science · June Week 3

Age 9

Science Fair & Year-End Review
🎯 Learning Goal

Organize all elements of the scientific method into a clear, attractive display board and practice presenting findings to an audience.

☀️ Warm-Up

Show examples of real science fair display boards. Notice how the best ones have clear headings, neat graphs, and photos of the experiment.
