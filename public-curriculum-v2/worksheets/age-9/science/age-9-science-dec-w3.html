Fossils and Earth's History

Age 9 Science · December Week 3

Age 9

🎯 Learning Goal

Understand how fossils form and explain what fossils tell us about organisms and environments that existed long ago.

☀️ Warm-Up

Show a picture of a dinosaur skeleton. How do we know these animals existed millions of years ago if no human was there to see them?
