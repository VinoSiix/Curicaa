🎯 Learning Goal

Trace the process of plant reproduction from pollination to seed formation to seed dispersal, and demonstrate different dispersal methods.

☀️ Warm-Up

Cut open an apple or orange. Find the seeds. How did this fruit get here? Why does it have seeds inside?
