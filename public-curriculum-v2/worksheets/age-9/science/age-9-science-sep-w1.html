Energy & Its Forms
🎯 Learning Goal

Define energy and identify the five main forms: kinetic, potential, thermal, electrical, and light energy. Understand that energy makes things happen.

☀️ Warm-Up

Sit completely still for 30 seconds. Then jump up and down! Discuss: "Where did the energy come from to make you move?"

✅ Can Your Child...?
  • Define what energy is in their own words
  • Name all five forms of energy
  • Give a real-world example for at least three energy forms
  • Explain that some objects have multiple energy types at once
💡 Easier: Focus on three forms only — kinetic, potential, and light.
💡 Harder: Introduce the Law of Conservation of Energy — energy cannot be created or destroyed, only changed from one form to another.
