Static Electricity Lab

Age 9 Science · October Week 1

Age 9

Electricity & Magnetism
🎯 Learning Goal

Demonstrate static electricity by transferring electrons between objects and explain that like charges repel and opposite charges attract.

☀️ Warm-Up

Rub a balloon on your hair or a wool sweater for 30 seconds. Hold it near small pieces of torn paper. Watch the paper jump! What invisible force is pulling them?
