Renewable and Nonrenewable Resources

Age 9 Science · May Week 1

Age 9

Natural Resources & Human Impact
🎯 Learning Goal

Classify natural resources as renewable (can be replaced in a human lifetime) or nonrenewable (finite supply), and understand why this distinction matters.

☀️ Warm-Up

Show an apple. Will we ever run out of apples? No, we can grow more — it is renewable. Now show a piece of coal. Can we make more coal? Not in our lifetime — it takes millions of years!
