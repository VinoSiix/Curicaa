Age 9 Science · February Week 4

Plant Structures & Functions
🎯 Learning Goal

Identify how plants are adapted to survive in specific environments (desert, rainforest, aquatic) by examining their structural features.

☀️ Warm-Up

Show pictures of a cactus and a water lily. Why does the cactus have needles instead of wide leaves? Why does the water lily have flat, floating leaves?
