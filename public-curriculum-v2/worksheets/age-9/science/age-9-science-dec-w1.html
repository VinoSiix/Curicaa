Age 9 Science · December Week 1

🎯 Learning Goal

Demonstrate how water, wind, and ice break down rocks (weathering) and move the pieces elsewhere (erosion) through hands-on models.

☀️ Warm-Up

Look at a photo of the Grand Canyon. What force on Earth could carve such a massive hole? Hint: it took millions of years and a river.

💡 Harder: Introduce deposition — when eroded material settles somewhere new, like a river delta.
