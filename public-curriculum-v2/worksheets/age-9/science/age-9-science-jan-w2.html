Groundwater, Aquifers, and Wells

Age 9 Science · January Week 2

Age 9

Earth's Water & Atmosphere
🎯 Learning Goal

Model how water moves underground through layers of earth and how wells access this groundwater.

☀️ Warm-Up

When it rains, where does the water go that does not run into rivers? Pour a cup of water onto a garden bed and watch it soak into the ground.

🔬 Activity: Build an Aquifer Model
