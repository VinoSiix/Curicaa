The Rock Cycle and Three Rock Types

Age 9 Science · December Week 2

Age 9

Earth's Surface Features
🎯 Learning Goal

Identify the three types of rocks (igneous, sedimentary, metamorphic) and understand how they transform into each other through the rock cycle.

☀️ Warm-Up

Collect 5-6 rocks from your yard. Look at them closely — are they layered, bubbly, shiny, or streaked with crystals? Every rock tells a story.

🔬 Activity: Crayon Rock Cycle Model

Model the rock cycle using crayons and record each step:
