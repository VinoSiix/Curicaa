Waves & Information Transfer
🎯 Learning Goal

Understand that patterns in waves can carry information and that humans use patterns to code and transmit messages.

☀️ Warm-Up

Flash the room lights on and off in a pattern: short-short-long. Could you send a message just by turning lights on and off?
