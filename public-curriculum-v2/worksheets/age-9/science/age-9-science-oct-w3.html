Age 9

Electricity & Magnetism
🎯 Learning Goal

Test various materials to classify them as electrical conductors or insulators and identify patterns in which materials conduct electricity.

☀️ Warm-Up

Look at a power cord. Why is the outside made of rubber but the inside is metal? Some materials let electricity through — others block it!
