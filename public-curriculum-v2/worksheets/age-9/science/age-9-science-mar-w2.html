🎯 Learning Goal

Identify key internal structures in animals and understand how bones, muscles, and organs work together as systems.

☀️ Warm-Up

Feel your forearm. The hard lumps are bones. The soft tissue on top is muscle. Your heart is an organ made of special muscle. Your body is a system of systems!
