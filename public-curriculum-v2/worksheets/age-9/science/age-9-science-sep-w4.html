Sound Energy and Vibrations

Age 9 Science · September Week 4

Age 9

Energy & Its Forms
🎯 Learning Goal

Demonstrate that sound is produced by vibrations and that sound energy travels through materials (solids, liquids, and gases).

☀️ Warm-Up

Place your hand on your throat and hum. Feel the vibrations! That buzzing feeling is your vocal cords vibrating to create sound.

💡 Harder: Discuss how sound travels faster through solids than air because molecules are closer together.
