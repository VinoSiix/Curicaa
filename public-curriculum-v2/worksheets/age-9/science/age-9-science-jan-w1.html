🎯 Learning Goal

Model and explain the four stages of the water cycle: evaporation, condensation, precipitation, and collection, using a hands-on terrarium.

☀️ Warm-Up

Hold a cold glass of ice water on a warm day. Where did the water drops on the outside come from? The water did not leak — it condensed from the air!

  • Explain what happens at each stage
