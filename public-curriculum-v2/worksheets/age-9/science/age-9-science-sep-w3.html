Conservation of Energy in Everyday Systems

Age 9 Science · September Week 3

Age 9

Energy & Its Forms
🎯 Learning Goal

Trace energy as it changes forms in a system, understanding that the total amount of energy stays the same (it is conserved).

☀️ Warm-Up

Hold a pencil above the table. Ask: "What kind of energy does it have right now?" Drop it. "Now what kind of energy?" The potential energy became kinetic energy!

💡 Easier: Focus on one simple chain with two steps (battery → light).
💡 Harder: Include "wasted" energy in the chain and discuss efficiency — what percentage of a light bulb's energy becomes useful light?
