Age 9 Science · January Week 3

Age 9

Earth's Water & Atmosphere
🎯 Learning Goal

Understand that ocean water is salty (salinity), that ocean currents are driven by temperature and wind, and that currents affect climate.

☀️ Warm-Up

Taste a tiny drop of salt water (1 tsp salt in a cup of water). Discuss why the ocean is salty but rivers and lakes are not.
