Energy Transfer in Collisions

Age 9 Science · September Week 2

Age 9

Energy & Its Forms
🎯 Learning Goal

Observe how energy transfers between objects during collisions and understand that speed and mass affect collision outcomes.

☀️ Warm-Up

Roll a marble gently into a line of five marbles. Watch what happens to the marbles at the end. Discuss: "Where did the energy go?"

🔬 Activity: Ramp Collision Experiment

Roll different balls down a ramp into a plastic cup. Measure how far the cup slides. Record your data:

💡 Easier: Compare only a light ball vs. a heavy ball without measuring distance.
