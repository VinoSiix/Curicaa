🎯 Learning Goal

Apply the Engineering Design Process to design a solution for a specific environmental problem, combining science knowledge with engineering skills.

☀️ Warm-Up

Show a picture of a landfill or ocean plastic pollution. If you were an engineer, what would you design to help solve this problem?
