Engineering Design Process
🎯 Learning Goal

Learn the first step of the Engineering Design Process: identifying a problem, defining criteria for success, and recognizing constraints.

☀️ Warm-Up

Look around the room and find one thing that is annoying or could work better. Congratulations — you just found an engineering problem!

✅ Can Your Child...?
  • Write a clear problem statement
  • Define at least two criteria for success
  • Identify at least one constraint

💡 Easier: Provide a pre-written problem and identify criteria and constraints.
💡 Harder: Define your own problem, set criteria and constraints, and justify why those limits are reasonable.
