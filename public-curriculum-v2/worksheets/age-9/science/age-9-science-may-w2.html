🎯 Learning Goal

Understand how burning fossil fuels affects the air, water, and climate, and compare the environmental impact of different energy sources.

☀️ Warm-Up

Look out the window on a busy road. Can you see exhaust from cars? That is pollution from burning gasoline, which is made from oil (a fossil fuel).
