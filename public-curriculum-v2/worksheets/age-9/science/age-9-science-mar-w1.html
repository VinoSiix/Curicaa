Animal Structures & Senses
🎯 Learning Goal

Compare how different animals sense their environment using specialized sensory organs and understand that different animals process information differently.

☀️ Warm-Up

Close your eyes. Have someone hold different foods near your nose. Can you identify them by smell alone? Now plug your nose and taste apple vs. potato — they taste the same without smell!
