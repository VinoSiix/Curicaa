Engineering Design Process
🎯 Learning Goal

Generate multiple possible solutions through brainstorming, then select the best idea and create a detailed plan.

☀️ Warm-Up

Set a timer for 2 minutes. Write down as many uses for a paperclip as you can think of. No idea is too wild! This is brainstorming — quantity over quality.

✅ Can Your Child...?
  • Generate at least 4 distinct solutions
  • Evaluate each against criteria
  • Select the best design with reasoning
  • Create a labeled blueprint with materials list
💡 Easier: Provide three partially-drawn ideas and choose one.
💡 Harder: Introduce trade-offs — a design that meets one criterion may sacrifice another.
