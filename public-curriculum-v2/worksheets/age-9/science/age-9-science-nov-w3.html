Light: Reflection, Refraction, and Shadows

Age 9 Science · November Week 3

Age 9

Waves & Information Transfer
🎯 Learning Goal

Demonstrate that light travels in straight lines, bounces off surfaces (reflection), bends through different materials (refraction), and is blocked by opaque objects (shadows).

☀️ Warm-Up

Shine a flashlight at a mirror on the table. Where does the light beam go? Trace the beam's path with your finger.

🔬 Activity: Light Behavior Stations

Visit each station and record your observations:

2. Refraction (pencil)Put pencil in glass of water

5. Shadows (light far)Flashlight far from toy

✅ Can Your Child...?
  • Demonstrate reflection using a mirror
  • Show refraction with the pencil-in-water experiment
  • Explain that shadows form when light is blocked
  • Describe how shadow size changes with distance
💡 Easier: Focus on reflection and shadows only.
💡 Harder: Introduce the term 'medium' — light bends because it changes speed when entering a new medium like water or glass.
