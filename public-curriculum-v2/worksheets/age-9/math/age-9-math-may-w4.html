🎯 Learning Goal

Apply all Grade 4 math skills in an integrated, real-world project: the Dream Bedroom Design.

☀️ Warm-Up

Brainstorm: If you could design your dream bedroom, what math skills would you need?

🧮 Project: Dream Bedroom Design

Complete each day's task on graph paper:

Day 1: Draw a scaled floor plan (1 square = 1 foot). Room: 12 ft × 14 ft. Calculate area and perimeter.

Day 2: Choose furniture with given dimensions. Place on floor plan. Calculate remaining floor space.

Day 3: Budget $500. Price paint (perimeter × wall height), flooring (area), and one special item. Calculate total and change.

Day 4: Add geometry: lines of symmetry in rug designs, measure and label 4 angles, classify furniture shapes.
