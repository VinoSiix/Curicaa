Fraction Operations
🎯 Learning Goal

Add fractions with like denominators by adding the numerators while keeping the denominator the same. Understand addition as joining parts of the same whole.

  • Add like-denominator fractions correctly
  • Explain why denominator stays the same
  • Recognize when sum equals one whole

💡 Easier: Use visual fraction pieces for halves, thirds, fourths.
