🎯 Learning Goal

Compare two fractions with different numerators and denominators by creating common denominators or comparing to a benchmark like 1/2. Record using >, <, or =.

☀️ Warm-Up

Quick benchmark: Is 3/8 greater or less than 1/2? Is 5/6 greater or less than 1/2?

✅ Can Your Child...?

  • Order fractions from least to greatest
