☀️ Warm-Up

🧮 The CER Framework

For every explanation:

  1. Claim — State your answer clearly
  2. Evidence — Show the math work
  3. Reasoning — Explain why the work is correct

Example: Is 3/4 greater than 2/3?

Claim: Yes, 3/4 is greater than 2/3.
Evidence: 3/4 = 9/12 and 2/3 = 8/12.
Reasoning: I converted to a common denominator of 12. Since 9 > 8, 9/12 > 8/12, so 3/4 > 2/3.
