Math Portfolio Presentation

Age 9 Math · June Week 4

Age 9

Portfolio & Celebration
🎯 Learning Goal

Organize and present a comprehensive math portfolio demonstrating growth and mastery across all Grade 4 standards.

☀️ Warm-Up

Spread all your math work on a table. Pick your top 5 favorite pieces!

🧮 Activity: Organize Your Portfolio

Create 6 sections in a binder:

  1. Number & Operations — Place value, multi-digit computation
  2. Fractions & Decimals — Fraction wall, budget project
  3. Geometry & Measurement — Geometry city, measurement lab
  4. Algebraic Thinking — Pattern museum, equations
  5. Problem Solving — Dream bedroom, best CER writing
  6. My Math Autobiography
✏️ Practice: Presentation Reflection

Write a short reflection for each section:

1. Number & Operations: What I learned, what I'm proud of.
2. Fractions & Decimals: My biggest "aha" moment.
3. Geometry & Measurement: How I see math in the world now.
4. Algebraic Thinking: A pattern or puzzle I enjoyed.
5. Problem Solving: A problem that challenged me and how I solved it.
📝 Practice: Presentation Prep
6. Write one sentence about your FAVORITE math topic this year.
7. Write one sentence about a skill you improved the most.
8. Write your math goal for next year.
9. Draw or decorate the cover of your math portfolio.
10. Create an award certificate for yourself! Category: _______________
✅ Can Your Child...?
  • Organize a complete math portfolio
  • Write reflections for each section
  • Prepare a 5-minute presentation
  • Celebrate mathematical growth!
💡 Easier: Focus on 3 sections with dictated reflections.
💡 Harder: Include a section analyzing a mistake and what was learned.
