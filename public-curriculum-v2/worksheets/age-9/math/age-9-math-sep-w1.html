✅ Can Your Child...?
  • Read a number in the millions aloud correctly
  • Identify the value of a digit in any place up to millions
  • Explain that a digit one place to the left is worth 10 times more
  • Write numbers in expanded, standard, and word form
💡 Easier: Start with numbers through thousands only. Use a physical place value chart with manipulatives.
💡 Harder: Convert between standard, expanded, and word form for numbers in the ten millions.
