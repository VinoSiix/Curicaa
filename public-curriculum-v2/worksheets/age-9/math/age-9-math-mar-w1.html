Customary & Metric Measurement Conversion

Age 9 Math · March Week 1

Age 9

Measurement & Data
🎯 Learning Goal

Know relative sizes of measurement units and express measurements in a larger unit in terms of a smaller unit.

☀️ Warm-Up

Hold up a gallon jug and a cup. How many cups fit in a gallon? (Answer: 16!)

✏️ Practice: Customary Conversions

Convert:

1. 4 feet = ___ inches
2. 3 yards = ___ feet
3. 5 pounds = ___ ounces
4. 2 gallons = ___ quarts
5. 3 quarts = ___ pints
6. 6 cups = ___ ounces
🔢 Practice: Metric Conversions

Convert:

7. 3 km = ___ meters
8. 4 kg = ___ grams
9. 5 L = ___ mL
10. 700 cm = ___ meters
11. 8,000 g = ___ kg
12. 2,500 mL = ___ L
✅ Can Your Child...?
  • Convert customary units (length, weight, capacity)
  • Convert metric units (length, mass, capacity)
  • Show the multiplication step for each conversion
  • Use conversion in real-world contexts
💡 Easier: Focus on metric length only.
💡 Harder: Two-step conversions like inches in 2 miles.
