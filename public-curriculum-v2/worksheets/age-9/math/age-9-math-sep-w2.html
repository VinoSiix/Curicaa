✅ Can Your Child...?
  • Compare two large numbers using >, <, or =
  • Round a number to any specified place
  • Explain which digit they looked at to decide whether to round up or down
  • Order three or more large numbers from least to greatest
💡 Easier: Round only to the nearest ten and hundred using numbers under 10,000 with a drawn number line.
💡 Harder: Round the same number to multiple places in sequence and discuss how the answer changes.
