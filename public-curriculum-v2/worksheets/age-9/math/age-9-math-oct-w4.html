Fraction Concepts & Equivalence
🎯 Learning Goal

Represent fractions on a number line by partitioning into equal parts. Decompose a fraction into a sum of fractions with the same denominator.

☀️ Warm-Up

Draw a number line from 0 to 1. Mark where 1/4, 2/4, 3/4, and 4/4 go.

✅ Can Your Child...?
  • Place fractions on a number line correctly
  • Decompose a fraction into unit fractions
  • Convert between improper fractions and mixed numbers
  • Relate fractions greater than 1 to mixed numbers
💡 Easier: Fractions from 0 to 1 using halves, thirds, and fourths.
💡 Harder: Place mixed numbers and improper fractions on the same line.
