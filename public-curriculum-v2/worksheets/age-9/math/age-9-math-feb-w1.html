Points, Lines, Line Segments & Rays

Age 9 Math · February Week 1

Age 9

Geometry
🎯 Learning Goal

Draw and identify points, lines, line segments, rays, and angles. Understand perpendicular and parallel lines.

☀️ Warm-Up

Use two pencils to show: crossed, parallel, touching at a point. Describe what you see.

✏️ Practice: Geometry Vocabulary

Match each term to its definition:

1. _____ : a dot marking a location (A, B, C)
2. _____ : extends infinitely in both directions (arrows on both ends)
3. _____ : has two endpoints, a portion of a line
4. _____ : one endpoint, extends infinitely in one direction
5. _____ : two lines that never cross, same distance apart
6. _____ : two lines that cross at a right angle (90°)
📝 Practice: Identify & Draw
7. Draw and label: point A, line segment AB, ray AC.
8. Are railroad tracks parallel or perpendicular?  
9. The corner of a book: parallel or perpendicular?  
10. Name something in your house with parallel lines: _______________
11. Name something with perpendicular lines: _______________
12. Draw two intersecting lines that are NOT perpendicular.
✅ Can Your Child...?
  • Define and draw point, line, line segment, ray
  • Identify parallel and perpendicular lines
  • Find real-world examples of each geometry term
  • Label geometric figures correctly
💡 Easier: Focus on points, lines, and line segments using physical objects.
💡 Harder: Discuss intersecting lines and the angles formed.
