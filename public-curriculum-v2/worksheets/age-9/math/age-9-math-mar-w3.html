Age 9 Math · March Week 3

Age 9

Measurement & Data
🎯 Learning Goal

Make a line plot to display measurements in fractions of a unit. Solve problems using information from line plots.

☀️ Warm-Up

Measure 8 pencils to the nearest quarter inch. Record on a sticky note.

✏️ Practice: Create a Line Plot

Measure 10 objects to the nearest half inch. Draw a number line and mark each measurement with an X above it.
