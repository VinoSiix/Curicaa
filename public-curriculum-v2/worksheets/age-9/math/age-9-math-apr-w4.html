Number & Shape Patterns

Age 9 Math · April Week 4

Age 9

Algebraic Thinking & Patterns
🎯 Learning Goal

Generate a number or shape pattern that follows a given rule. Identify features not explicit in the rule.

☀️ Warm-Up

What comes next? 2, 6, 18, 54, ___. (Multiply by 3 each time.)

✏️ Practice: Continue the Pattern

Write the next 3 numbers and state the rule:

1. 5, 8, 11, 14, ___, ___, ___ Rule: _______________
2. 2, 4, 8, 16, ___, ___, ___ Rule: _______________
3. 100, 95, 90, 85, ___, ___, ___ Rule: _______________
4. 3, 5, 9, 17, ___, ___, ___ Rule: ×2 then −1
🔢 Practice: Is It In the Pattern?

Start at 7, add 4 each time: 7, 11, 15, 19, 23...

5. Will 50 ever appear? Show why or why not.  
6. Will 99 ever appear?  
7. What is the 10th term?  
8. What is the 25th term?  
📝 Practice: Shape Patterns
9. 1 triangle = 3 toothpicks. 2 triangles sharing a side = 5. 3 = 7. Rule: 2n + 1. For 10 triangles?  
10. Squares: 1 square = 4 sticks. 2 sharing a side = 7. 3 = 10. Rule: ___n + ___. For 20 squares?  
11. Create your own pattern with a rule: ___________________________________
12. Fibonacci: 1, 1, 2, 3, 5, 8, ___, ___, ___. Rule: _______________
✅ Can Your Child...?
  • Continue number patterns and state the rule
  • Determine if a number is in a pattern
  • Find the nth term of a pattern
  • Create original patterns with rules
💡 Easier: Simple growing patterns with addition only.
💡 Harder: Two-step rules and general expressions for the nth term.
