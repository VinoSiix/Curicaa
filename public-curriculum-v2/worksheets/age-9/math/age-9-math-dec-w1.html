Decimal Notation
🎯 Learning Goal

Use decimal notation for fractions with denominators of 10. Each digit right of the decimal represents a fractional part of a whole.

☀️ Warm-Up

Divide a rectangle into 10 equal strips. Shade 3. Write as fraction (3/10) and decimal (0.3).
