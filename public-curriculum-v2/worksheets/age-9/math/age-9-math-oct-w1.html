Understanding Fractions as Equal Parts

Age 9 Math · October Week 1

Age 9

Fraction Concepts & Equivalence
🎯 Learning Goal

Understand a fraction as a number representing equal parts of a whole, where the numerator counts the parts and the denominator shows how many equal parts make the whole.

☀️ Warm-Up

Fold a piece of paper in half, then in half again. Open it. What fraction is one section? Two sections?

✏️ Practice: Equal Parts?

Circle YES if equal parts, NO if not:

1. Rectangle cut into 4 pieces of different sizes. Equal?  
2. Circle divided into 3 identical slices. Equal?  
3. Square cut diagonally into 2 triangles. Equal?  
4. Pie with 4 large and 4 tiny slices. Equal?

💡 Easier: Focus on halves, thirds, and fourths using paper strips.
💡 Harder: Fractions of a set — what fraction of 12 eggs is 3?
