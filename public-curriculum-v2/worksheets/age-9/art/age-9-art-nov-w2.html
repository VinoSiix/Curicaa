Papier-mache Construction

Age 9 Art & PE · November Week 2

Age 9

Sculpture
🎯 Learning Goal

Learn the papier-mache process -- building an armature, layering paste and paper, and painting the finished form.

☀️ Warm-Up

Tear newspaper into strips of various sizes (roughly 2cm x 15cm). Practice the dipping-and-wiping motion with dry strips on a balloon.

✏️ Activity: Papier-mache Construction

Step 1: Inflate a balloon, tape on cardboard features. Step 2: Mix paste. Step 3: Dip strips, wipe excess, lay flat on armature. Step 4: Cover with 3 layers. Step 5: Let dry. Step 6: Paint and add details.
