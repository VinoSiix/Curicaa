🎯 Learning Goal

Learn three composition rules -- rule of thirds, framing, and leading lines -- and practice them with a camera or phone.

☀️ Warm-Up

Frame Finder: Make a rectangle with your thumbs and index fingers. Look through it at objects around the room.

📷 Activity: Photography Composition Basics

Rule of Thirds: Place subject on intersection of imaginary grid. Framing: Use doorway/window to frame subject. Leading Lines: Use roads/fences to draw the eye. Take 3 photos of each technique (9 total).

💡 Tip: Easier: Focus on rule of thirds only with camera grid overlay. Harder: Add symmetry, negative space, and fill the frame.
