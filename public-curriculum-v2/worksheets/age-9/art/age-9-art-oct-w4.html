Teamwork & Modified Game Play

Age 9 Art & PE · October Week 4

Age 9

Team Sports
🎯 Learning Goal

Apply soccer skills in a modified game setting while practicing communication, positioning, and sportsmanship.

☀️ Warm-Up

Partner Pass Challenge: Stand 3m apart and see how many consecutive one-touch passes your pair can complete.

🥇 Activity: Teamwork & Modified Game Play

Play a modified 3v3 or 4v4 soccer game. Rules: No goalkeepers, goals from shooting zone only, call for the ball by name, rotate positions every 5 minutes. After the game, share one thing a teammate did well.

🥇 Practice: Sportsmanship Reflection

After the game, reflect on teamwork and sportsmanship:

1. I passed to a teammate ___ times.

2. One thing a teammate did well:

3. One example of good sportsmanship I saw:

4. Communication: I called for the ball by saying:

💡 Reflection Questions

1. What was the most challenging part of this week's activity?

2. What would you do differently next time?

✅ Can Your Child...?
  • Demonstrated correct foot placement for dribbling, passing, and shooting
  • Passed to a teammate at least 3 times during the game
  • Communicated verbally during the game
  • Named one example of good sportsmanship
💡 Tip: Easier: Play 2v2 with a larger goal. Harder: Every player must touch the ball before a goal counts.
