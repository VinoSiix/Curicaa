Blind Contour Drawing

Age 9 Art & PE · September Week 1

Age 9

Drawing
🎯 Learning Goal

Train the eye to observe edges carefully by drawing objects without looking at the paper.

☀️ Warm-Up

Hand-eye warmup: Trace the outline of your non-dominant hand in the air with your dominant index finger, slowly following every bump and curve.

🎨 Activity: Blind Contour Drawing

Place a shoe or a crumpled ball of paper on the table. Set a timer for 5 minutes. Draw the object using a single continuous line without lifting the pen or looking at the paper. Focus entirely on the edges you see.

✏️ Practice: Blind Contour Sketch Boxes

Set a timer for 3 minutes per box. Pick an object nearby and draw it WITHOUT looking at the paper. Use one continuous line.

Object 1: _______________

Reflection: Which drawing captured more details? What did you notice about the object that you would have missed otherwise?

1. What was the most challenging part of this week's activity?

2. What would you do differently next time?

✅ Can Your Child...?
  • Kept eyes on the object during blind contour drawing
  • Identified specific details noticed during observation
  • Created shading showing a visible light source and shadow
  • Can name all four shading techniques (hatching, cross-hatching, stippling, blending)
💡 Tip: Easier: Use a simpler object like a mug or an apple. Harder: Try a modified contour where you peek briefly every 30 seconds to adjust placement.
