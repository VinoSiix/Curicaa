Portfolio Compilation & Self-Assessment

Age 9 Art & PE · June Week 1

Age 9

Portfolio
🎯 Learning Goal

Compile a year-long art and PE portfolio, self-assess growth, and reflect on learning across both subjects.

☀️ Warm-Up

Memory Map: Draw a quick sketch map of the school year -- label each month with one art project and one physical activity you remember.

📚 Activity: Portfolio Compilation & Self-Assessment

Create a portfolio binder: Art section (8-10 best artworks), PE section (fitness results, activity logs). Complete a Self-Assessment Sheet answering 6 reflection questions about growth and goals.

📚 Practice: Self-Assessment

Complete your year-end self-assessment:

1. My strongest art skill now is:

2. The art skill I improved the most is:

3. My physical activity I enjoy most is:

4. My September fitness goal was:

5. I achieved / am still working toward it.

6. One thing I'm most proud of this year:

7. One thing I want to work on next year:

💡 Reflection Questions

1. What was the most challenging part of this week's activity?

2. What would you do differently next time?

✅ Can Your Child...?
  • Completed a neatly organized portfolio with art and PE sections
  • Wrote thoughtful self-assessment responses
  • Independently planned and completed a creative project
  • Can verbalize specific areas of growth
💡 Tip: Easier: Self-assess orally with parent guidance. Harder: Write a 1-page Year in Review essay with specific examples.
