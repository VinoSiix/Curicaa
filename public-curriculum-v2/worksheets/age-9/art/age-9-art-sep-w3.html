Age 9 Art & PE · September Week 3

Age 9

Fitness
🎯 Learning Goal

Measure current fitness levels in four areas -- muscular strength, endurance, flexibility, and cardiovascular capacity -- and set personal improvement goals.

☀️ Warm-Up

March in place for 1 minute, then do 10 arm circles forward and 10 backward to warm up joints and muscles.

🏃 Activity: Baseline Fitness Testing

Conduct four fitness tests: Test 1: Push-ups (proper form in 1 minute). Test 2: Curl-ups/crunches (1 minute). Test 3: Sit-and-reach flexibility. Test 4: Timed 6-minute run. Write down all results and set one specific goal for each test by June.
