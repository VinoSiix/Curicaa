🎯 Learning Goal

Organize and participate in a multi-event sports festival, applying all physical skills learned throughout the year.

☀️ Warm-Up

Year in Movement warmup: 30 seconds each of jogging, dribbling, crab walk, jumping jacks, balance pose, grapevine, bear crawl, sprint, stretch.

🏃 Activity: Sports Festival: Multi-Event Celebration

Host a Sports Festival with 6 events: 50m sprint, standing long jump, softball throw, balance beam walk, soccer dribble, yoga pose sequence. Record all results on a Festival Scorecard. Award medals.
