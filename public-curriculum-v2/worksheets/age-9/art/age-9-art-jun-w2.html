🎯 Learning Goal

Plan and execute an independent creative project using any technique or material learned during the year, demonstrating self-direction.

☀️ Warm-Up

Technique Brainstorm: List every art technique you learned on index cards. Lay them out. Pick your top 3 favorites.

📚 Activity: Artist's Choice: Independent Project

Choose your technique. Sketch your idea, list materials, estimate time (30-60 min). Create the project independently. Write a project report: What did you make? Technique used? What was challenging? What would you change?
