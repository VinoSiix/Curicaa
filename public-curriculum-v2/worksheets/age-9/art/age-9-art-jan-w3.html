Balance & Body Control

Age 9 Art & PE · January Week 3

Age 9

Gymnastics
🎯 Learning Goal

Develop static and dynamic balance through beam walking, one-foot stands, and balance poses.

☀️ Warm-Up

Stand on one foot for 15 seconds, then switch. Try it with eyes closed for 10 seconds.

🏃 Activity: Balance & Body Control

5 balance challenges: (1) Heel-to-toe walk on tape. (2) Walk on low beam carrying a book. (3) One-foot stand with movements. (4) Clock Balance. (5) Partner lean. Discuss core strength as key to balance.
