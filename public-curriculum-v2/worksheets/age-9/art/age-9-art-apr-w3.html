Track Events: Sprinting & Pacing

Age 9 Art & PE · April Week 3

Age 9

Track & Field
🎯 Learning Goal

Learn correct sprinting technique (drive phase, arm action, relaxation) and practice pacing for middle-distance running.

☀️ Warm-Up

A-Skip/B-Skip drills: High knee skips emphasizing driving the knee up and pushing off the ball of the foot. 2x15m of each.

🏃 Activity: Track Events: Sprinting & Pacing

Sprint technique: Start position, Drive Phase (first 10m), Acceleration (10-30m), Top Speed. Run 4x30m sprints. Then run 400m at 70% effort, rest, run 400m at 90%. Compare times.
