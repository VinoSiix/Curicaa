🎯 Learning Goal

Learn proper running form -- arm swing, foot strike, and breathing -- and practice pacing for distance running.

☀️ Warm-Up

High knees for 20 seconds, butt kicks for 20 seconds, then gentle jogging in place for 30 seconds.

🏃 Activity: Running Technique & Pacing

Practice running form: (1) Arms bent at 90 degrees. (2) Land on the midfoot. (3) Lean slightly forward from the ankles. (4) Breathe in for 2 steps, out for 2 steps. Run a measured 400m loop at a talk pace -- you should be able to speak full sentences.

🏃 Practice: Running Form Checklist

Practice each element of running form. Check off each one when you've practiced it correctly.

  • Arms bent at 90 degrees, swinging forward and back
  • Landing on midfoot (not heel)
  • Leaning slightly forward from the ankles
  • Breathing in for 2 steps, out for 2 steps
