Soccer Fundamentals

Age 9 Art & PE · October Week 3

Age 9

Team Sports
🎯 Learning Goal

Learn and practice three core soccer skills: dribbling, passing, and shooting with proper technique.

☀️ Warm-Up

Jog around the playing area, stopping every 30 seconds to do 5 toe touches and 5 heel raises.

🥇 Activity: Soccer Fundamentals

(1) Dribbling: Use inside of foot, weave through 5 cones. (2) Passing: Partner up 5m apart, inside of foot, 10 accurate passes. (3) Shooting: Set up a goal 8m away, strike with laces, 10 shots, count goals.

✅ Can Your Child...?
  • Demonstrated correct foot placement for dribbling, passing, and shooting
  • Passed to a teammate at least 3 times during the game
  • Communicated verbally during the game
  • Named one example of good sportsmanship
💡 Tip: Easier: Use a larger, softer ball; reduce passing distance. Harder: Add a defender who applies light pressure.
