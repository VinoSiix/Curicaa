🎯 Learning Goal

Create repeated prints using foam plates, vegetables, or cardboard, understanding positive and negative space.

☀️ Warm-Up

Press your thumb onto paint, then stamp onto paper. Use a fine marker to turn each thumbprint into a tiny character. Make 5 thumbprint characters.

✏️ Activity: Printmaking with Found Objects

Option A: Foam Plate Printing -- Draw into foam with blunt pencil, apply paint, press paper on top. Option B: Vegetable Stamping -- Carve shape into potato, dip in paint, create patterns. Discuss positive vs negative space.

🎨 Practice: Print Design & Positive/Negative Space
