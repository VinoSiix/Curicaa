Age 9 Art & PE · March Week 4

Age 9

Outdoor
🎯 Learning Goal

Design and complete an outdoor obstacle course using natural and household materials, combining strength, agility, and problem-solving.

☀️ Warm-Up

Animal Movement Circuit: Bear crawl 10m, frog jump 5 times, crab walk 10m, bunny hop 5 times. Rest 30 seconds and repeat.

🏔️ Activity: Nature Obstacle Courses

Design 8 stations: balance beam walk, cone zigzag, hoop jumps, crawl tunnel, target throw, wall push, jump over rope, sprint finish. Time first run, then try to beat it. Discuss which required strength vs agility vs balance.
