🎯 Learning Goal

Learn 8 basic yoga poses and a simple breathing technique for flexibility, balance, and mental focus.

☀️ Warm-Up

Balloon Breath: Sit cross-legged. Inhale through nose 4 counts, hold 2, exhale through mouth 6 counts. Repeat 5 times.

🏃 Activity: Yoga & Mindful Stretching

Learn 8 poses: Mountain, Tree, Warrior I, Downward Dog, Cat-Cow, Cobra, Child's Pose, Happy Baby. Hold each 30 seconds per side. Flow through all 8 twice. End with Balloon Breath.

🧗️ Practice: Yoga Pose Tracker
