Age 9

Mixed Media
🎯 Learning Goal

Create a layered collage using papers of different textures, colors, and patterns to compose a unified artwork.

☀️ Warm-Up

Texture Hunt: Collect 5 different textured items and arrange them from smoothest to roughest.

✏️ Activity: Collage Techniques

Create a Seasons Collage: Fold paper into 4 quadrants. Each quadrant represents a season. Use torn magazine pages, tissue paper, foil, newspaper, fabric scraps, and drawn elements. Layer from background to foreground.
