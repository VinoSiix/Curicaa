Relay Races & Team Strategy

Age 9 Art & PE · November Week 4

Age 9

Teamwork
🎯 Learning Goal

Practice relay racing technique (baton passing) and develop team strategies for group competitions.

☀️ Warm-Up

Dynamic stretching: 30 seconds each of high knees, butt kicks, side shuffles, and carioca steps.

🥇 Activity: Relay Races & Team Strategy

Teach proper relay baton pass. Practice the exchange 10 times with a partner. Run full relay races with teams of 3-4. Try variations: egg-and-spoon, sack race, water bucket relay.
