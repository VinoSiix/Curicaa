Cardboard Loom Weaving

Age 9 Art & PE · February Week 1

Age 9

Textile Art
🎯 Learning Goal

Build a simple cardboard loom and weave a small mat using the over-under technique, creating patterns with color.

☀️ Warm-Up

Finger Weaving warmup: Interlock your fingers in different patterns -- alternate fingers, then two together, then one apart.

🌀 Activity: Cardboard Loom Weaving

Cut cardboard 15cm x 20cm with 8 slits top and bottom. Warp with yarn. Weft: over-under across, push snug, reverse next row. Change colors every 4-6 rows for stripes. Cut warp and tie pairs when done.

🖌️ Practice: Weaving Pattern Planner

Plan your weaving before starting. Color in the grid to show your pattern!

✅ Can Your Child...?
  • Built a functional cardboard loom
  • Completed weaving with consistent over-under technique
  • Created a visible pattern using 2+ colors
  • Can name the culture that inspired their pattern design
💡 Tip: Easier: Use a pre-made loom with 5-6 warp strings. Harder: Create a checkerboard or diagonal stripe pattern.
