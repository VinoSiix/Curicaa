Advanced Team-Building Challenges

Age 9 Art & PE · May Week 3

Age 9

Leadership
🎯 Learning Goal

Lead and participate in team challenges that require planning, delegation, and group problem-solving.

☀️ Warm-Up

Count to 20 game: Group counts to 20, each person says one number. If two speak at once, start over. No pre-planning.

🥇 Activity: Advanced Team-Building Challenges

Challenge 1: Marshmallow Tower (spaghetti, tape, marshmallow). Challenge 2: Toxic River (cross using 4 cardboard squares). Challenge 3: Silent Lineup (order by birthday without speaking). Rotate leader roles.

✅ Can Your Child...?
  • Took on a leadership role in at least one challenge
  • Delegated tasks clearly
  • Demonstrated fair play and encouraged teammates
  • Can articulate why sportsmanship matters more than winning
💡 Tip: Easier: Work in pairs; give hints during river challenge. Harder: Add budget constraint to tower challenge.
