Reading Goals Reflection & Summer Plan

June Week 2

Age 9

Portfolio & Reflection
🎯 Learning Goal

Reflect on reading growth throughout the year, set summer reading goals, and create a personalized summer reading plan.

☀️ Warm-Up: Reading Timeline

Create a timeline of the year, marking books you read each month with titles and a 1-5 star rating. Which month was your best? What was your favorite book?

📝 Activity: Three-Part Reflection
  1. Reading Inventory: How many books did you read? What genres? What was the longest? What surprised you?
  2. Goal Setting: Set 3 specific, measurable summer reading goals.
  3. Summer Reading Plan: Create a bucket list of 15 books across genres (realistic fiction, fantasy, biography, poetry, graphic novels, nonfiction).
