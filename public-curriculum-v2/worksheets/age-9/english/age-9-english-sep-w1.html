Building Reading Stamina

September Week 1

Age 9

Reading Foundations
🎯 Learning Goal

Read independently for 20-30 minutes and track reading habits in a log.

☀️ Warm-Up: Book Tasting

Set out 6-8 chapter books of different genres. Browse each book for 2 minutes, then pick one to start reading. Try: Tales of a Fourth Grade Nothing by Judy Blume or The One and Only Ivan by Katherine Applegate.

📝 Activity: Set Up Your Reading Log

Create a Reading Log with these columns: Date | Book Title | Pages Read | One Sentence Summary.

  1. Read silently for 20 minutes.
  2. After reading, fill in your log entry for today.
  3. Use the Five Finger Rule: read one page and count unknown words. 0-1 = too easy, 2-3 = just right, 4-5 = too hard.
✏️ Practice: Reading Habits & Genre Match

Part A: Match each genre to its description. Draw a line.

Part B: Apply the Five Finger Rule. Write the title of the book you tested and how many unknown words you found.

  2. Book title:   Unknown words:   Too easy / Just right / Too hard (circle one)
  3. Book title:   Unknown words:   Too easy / Just right / Too hard (circle one)

✍️ Writing Space
✅ Can Your Child...?
  • Read independently for at least 20 minutes without stopping
  • Complete a reading log entry with title, pages, and summary sentence
  • Use the Five Finger Rule to check if a book is "just right"
  • Identify at least 3 different book genres
💡 Easier: Start with 15 minutes of reading and allow an audiobook companion.
💡 Harder: Set a goal of 30 minutes and write a 3-sentence summary with character names and a plot event.
