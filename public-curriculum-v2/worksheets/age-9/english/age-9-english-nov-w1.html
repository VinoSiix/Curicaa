Forming & Supporting Opinions

November Week 1

Age 9

Opinion Writing
🎯 Learning Goal

State a clear opinion on a topic and support it with at least two reasons backed by evidence or examples.

☀️ Warm-Up: Would You Rather?

Read each "Would You Rather" dilemma. State your choice and give one reason.

  1. Would you rather have the ability to fly or be invisible?
  2. Would you rather live in the mountains or at the beach?
  3. Would you rather never eat pizza again or never eat ice cream again?
📝 Activity: The OREO Method

Learn the OREO method for opinion writing:

  1. O = Opinion: State it clearly
  2. R = Reason: Why do you think this?
  3. E = Example/Evidence: Give proof
  4. O = Opinion: Restate your opinion

Practice with the topic: "The best season of the year is ___."

✍️ Writing Space
✅ Can Your Child...?
  • Complete an OREO organizer with a clear opinion and 2 reasons with examples
  • Distinguish between facts and opinions correctly
  • Write all parts in complete sentences
  • Restate the opinion at the end
💡 Easier: Use a fill-in-the-blank OREO template and focus on one reason.
💡 Harder: Require 3 reasons and introduce addressing a counterargument ("Some people think ___, but I disagree because...").
