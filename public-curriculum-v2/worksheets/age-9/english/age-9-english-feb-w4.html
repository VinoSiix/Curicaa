Transition Words & Academic Vocabulary

February Week 4

Age 9

Informational Writing
🎯 Learning Goal

Use transition words and phrases to connect ideas within and between paragraphs; build academic vocabulary for informational writing.

☀️ Warm-Up: Transition Relay

Start: "First, we went to the store." Add: "Next, we bought ingredients." Keep the chain going for 10 sentences using different transitions!

📝 Activity: Transition Word Wall & Vocabulary

Create a Transition Word Wall with categories:

  • Time/Sequence: first, next, then, finally, meanwhile
  • Addition: also, furthermore, additionally
  • Compare/Contrast: however, on the other hand, similarly, in contrast
  • Cause/Effect: therefore, as a result, consequently, because
  • Conclusion: in conclusion, to summarize, overall, in summary

Also learn 10 academic vocabulary words: analyze, cite, demonstrate, distinguish, evidence, infer, relevant, summarize, support, thesis.
