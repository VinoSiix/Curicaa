Parts of Speech Deep Dive

January Week 2

Age 9

Grammar & Mechanics
🎯 Learning Goal

Identify and correctly use adverbs, prepositions, and conjunctions in writing; review nouns, verbs, and adjectives.

☀️ Warm-Up: Mad Libs

Complete a Mad Libs story by filling in the requested parts of speech. Notice how each blank asks for a specific type of word — and how the story changes based on your choices!

📝 Activity: Parts of Speech Reference

Review: Nouns (person/place/thing), Verbs (action), Adjectives (describe nouns).

New parts of speech:

  • Adverbs: Describe verbs, adjectives, or other adverbs. Often end in -ly. ("She ran quickly.")
  • Prepositions: Show time or place relationships (in, on, under, between, through, before, after).
  • Conjunctions: Joining words (and, but, or, so, because, although).
✏️ Practice: Parts of Speech

  4. She sits _____ Maria and Jose.
  5. _____ dinner, we watched a movie.
  6. Leave your shoes _____ the door.

✍️ Writing Space
✅ Can Your Child...?
  • Label parts of speech in a paragraph with 85% accuracy
  • Use adverbs correctly in original sentences
  • Use prepositions and conjunctions in writing
  • Identify all 6 parts of speech in context
💡 Easier: Focus on adverbs and conjunctions only, using a word bank.
💡 Harder: Introduce interjections and articles, and discuss coordinating vs. subordinating conjunctions.
