Vocabulary & Word Study
🎯 Learning Goal

Identify common Greek and Latin roots (geo, hydro, tele, bio, graph, phon, port, struct) and use them to determine the meaning of unknown words.

☀️ Warm-Up: Word Detective

"Telephone" = "tele" (far) + "phone" (sound) = "sound carried far!" Do the same for "television" (tele = far, vis = see). You can figure out words by knowing their parts!

📝 Activity: 8 Roots & Root Trees

Learn 8 high-value roots and create a Root Tree for each:

  • GEO (earth): geography, geology, geometry
  • HYDRO (water): hydroplane, dehydrate, hydraulic
  • TELE (far): telescope, television, teleport
  • BIO (life): biology, biography, biosphere
  • GRAPH (write/draw): autograph, photograph, paragraph
  • PHON (sound): phonics, symphony, microphone
  • PORT (carry): transport, portable, export
  • STRUCT (build): construct, structure, destruction
