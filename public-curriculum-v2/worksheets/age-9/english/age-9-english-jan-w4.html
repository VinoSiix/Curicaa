Grammar & Mechanics
🎯 Learning Goal

Apply spelling patterns for common suffixes (-tion, -sion, -ment, -ness), the "i before e" rule with exceptions, and commonly misspelled Grade 4 words.

☀️ Warm-Up: Spelling Bee Warm-Up

Try spelling these Grade 4 words aloud: available, beautiful, beginning, different, enough, especially, favorite, friend, important, knowledge. Celebrate every attempt!

📝 Activity: Three Spelling Patterns
  1. Suffixes: -tion/-sion say /shun/ (action, television), -ment makes nouns from verbs (enjoy → enjoyment), -ness makes nouns from adjectives (kind → kindness).
  2. "I before E" rule: "I before E except after C, or when sounding like A as in neighbor and weigh." Sort: believe, achieve, receive, ceiling, weigh, sleigh.
  3. Personal spelling list: 10 words you commonly misspell. Practice with Look-Say-Cover-Write-Check.
