November Week 3

Age 9

Persuasive Writing
🎯 Learning Goal

Identify and use persuasive techniques including emotional appeals, expert quotes, and strong word choice to convince a reader.

☀️ Warm-Up: Ad Detective

Think of a TV commercial or ad you've seen. What was it trying to sell? How did it try to convince you? Was there an emotion, an expert, or strong words?

📝 Activity: Three Persuasive Techniques

Learn three techniques to make your writing more convincing:

  1. Appeal to Emotion: Use words that make the reader feel something (imagine, terrifying, heartwarming)
  2. Expert Opinion: Quote or reference someone who knows the topic ("According to Dr. Smith, a veterinarian with 20 years of experience...")
  3. Strong Word Choice: Replace weak words with powerful ones (good → excellent, bad → harmful, big → enormous)
