Point of View & Perspective

March Week 3

Age 9

Literature Study
🎯 Learning Goal

Identify the narrator's point of view (first person, second person, third person limited, third person omniscient) and explain how perspective shapes how events are described.

☀️ Warm-Up: Same Event, Different Eyes

Describe dropping an ice cream cone from 3 perspectives: the kid who dropped it, the parent watching, and the dog who ate it. Same event, completely different story!

📝 Activity: Four Points of View
  • First Person: Narrator is a character. Uses I, me, my. ("I went to the store.")
  • Second Person: Speaks to the reader. Uses you. ("You walk into the dark room.")
  • Third Person Limited: Outside narrator knows one character's thoughts. Uses he, she, they. ("She felt nervous.")
  • Third Person Omniscient: Outside narrator knows ALL characters' thoughts. ("While Maria worried, Jose was excited.")
