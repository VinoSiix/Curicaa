Oral Presentation & Debate Skills

November Week 4

Age 9

Speaking & Listening
🎯 Learning Goal

Present an opinion speech clearly using eye contact, appropriate volume, and persuasive language; participate in a structured debate.

☀️ Warm-Up: Tongue Twisters

Say each tongue twister slowly, then fast: "She sells seashells by the seashore." "Peter Piper picked a peck of pickled peppers." "Red lorry, yellow lorry." This warms up your mouth and builds articulation!

📝 Activity: Speech & Debate Prep

Turn your opinion essay into a 2-minute speech. Practice these skills:

  • Stand tall with shoulders back
  • Make eye contact with at least 3 different listeners
  • Speak loudly enough to be heard across the room
  • Use hand gestures for emphasis
  • Pause after important points

Then hold a mini-debate with a family member on a fun topic!

✍️ Writing Space
✅ Can Your Child...?
  • Present a 2-minute speech with eye contact and audible volume
  • Use at least 2 persuasive techniques in the speech
  • Respond to at least one counter-question with evidence
  • Give specific feedback using "Two Stars and a Wish"
💡 Easier: Read from note cards with key words only (no full sentences).
💡 Harder: Present without any notes and include a visual aid (poster or prop).
