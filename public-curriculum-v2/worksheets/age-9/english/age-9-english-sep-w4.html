September Week 4

Age 9

Reading Comprehension
🎯 Learning Goal

Write thoughtful responses to reading that include opinions, text evidence, and personal connections.

☀️ Warm-Up: Quick Write

If you could be any character from a book you've read this month, who would you be and why? Write for 5 minutes without stopping.

📝 Activity: Three Types of Responses

Set up your Reading Response Journal. Learn three types of connections:

  1. Text-to-Self: "This reminds me of a time when I..."
  2. Text-to-Text: "This is like another book because..."
  3. Text-to-World: "This makes me think about real life because..."

After reading a chapter, write one full paragraph (5-7 sentences) using one of the three response types. Include at least one specific detail from the text.

✍️ Writing Space
✅ Can Your Child...?
  • Write a reading response naming a specific event from the text
  • Make a personal connection (Text-to-Self, Text-to-Text, or Text-to-World)
  • Explain thinking in at least 5 sentences
  • Include at least one specific text detail as evidence
💡 Easier: Use sentence starters like "I noticed that..." or "I felt surprised when..."
💡 Harder: Require two text details as evidence and write a counter-perspective ("On the other hand...").
