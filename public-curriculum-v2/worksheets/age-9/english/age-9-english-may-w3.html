Creative Writing
🎯 Learning Goal

Revise writing for content and organization (ideas, structure, word choice) and edit for conventions (spelling, punctuation, grammar) using a systematic checklist.

☀️ Warm-Up: Real Authors Revise

Roald Dahl rewrote Charlie and the Chocolate Factory multiple times. J.K. Rowling's first Harry Potter draft was rejected 12 times. Revision is NOT failure — it's the work of writing!

📝 Activity: ARMS & CUPS

ARMS (Revision — big changes):

  • Add: details, dialogue, descriptions
  • Remove: unnecessary words, repetitive ideas
  • Move: rearrange for better flow
  • Substitute: stronger words, better transitions

CUPS (Editing — small fixes):

  • Capitalization
  • Usage (grammar)
  • Punctuation
  • Spelling

✅ Can Your Child...?
  • Show marked-up drafts with ARMS changes
  • Complete a CUPS editing checklist
  • Give "Glow and Grow" feedback to a peer
  • Final draft shows measurable improvement over first draft
💡 Easier: Focus on one ARMS step and one CUPS step at a time.
💡 Harder: Revise, edit, and write a polished final copy, then compare first and final drafts side by side.
