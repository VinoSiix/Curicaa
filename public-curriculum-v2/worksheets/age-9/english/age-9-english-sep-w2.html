Story Elements: Characters, Setting, Plot

September Week 2

Age 9

Narrative Elements
🎯 Learning Goal

Identify and describe the main characters, setting, and plot structure (beginning, middle, end) in a fiction text.

☀️ Warm-Up: Character Quick-Think

Think of a character from a book or movie you know well. In 60 seconds, tell a family member: Who are they? Where do they live? What is their biggest problem?

📝 Activity: Story Map

After reading a chapter from your independent book, fill in the Story Map below. Think about: Who are the characters? Where and when does the story take place? What went wrong? What happened? How was it resolved?

✍️ Writing Space
✅ Can Your Child...?
  • Identify at least 2 characters in a text (main and minor)
  • Describe the setting with where AND when
  • State the main problem and solution in a story
  • Complete a Story Map for one chapter of a book
💡 Easier: Use a picture book with a clear, simple plot and fill in the Story Map together.
💡 Harder: Identify internal character traits (brave, curious, kind) in addition to external descriptions.
