Write a 5-paragraph opinion essay with an introduction, 3 body paragraphs (one for each reason), and a conclusion.

📝 Activity: 5-Paragraph Essay

Plan your opinion essay using the outline below:

  • Introduction: Hook + thesis statement (your opinion)
  • Body Paragraph 1: Reason 1 + evidence
  • Body Paragraph 2: Reason 2 + evidence
  • Body Paragraph 3: Reason 3 + evidence
  • Conclusion: Restate opinion + call to action or thought-provoking question

✍️ Writing Space
✅ Can Your Child...?
  • Write an introduction with a hook and clear opinion (thesis)
  • Write at least 2 body paragraphs each with a reason and supporting evidence
  • Write a conclusion that restates the opinion
  • Use complete sentences in every paragraph
💡 Easier: Write 3 paragraphs total (intro, 1 body, conclusion) with a provided topic sentence.
💡 Harder: Write the full 5-paragraph essay and include a counterargument paragraph.
