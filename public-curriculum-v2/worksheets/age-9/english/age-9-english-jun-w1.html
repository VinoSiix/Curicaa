June Week 1

Age 9

Portfolio & Reflection
🎯 Learning Goal

Select, organize, and reflect on writing pieces from the year to create a personal writing portfolio that demonstrates growth.

☀️ Warm-Up: Memory Lane

Spread out all your writing pieces from the year. Walk around and read through your work, noting how your writing has changed. What surprises you?

📝 Activity: Build Your Portfolio

Select 6-8 pieces that show variety and growth:

  • At least 1 personal narrative, 1 opinion essay, 1 informational piece, 1 poem, 1 creative story, 1 free choice
  • Write a Reflection Tag (2-3 sentences) for each: Why did you choose it? What did you learn? What would you do differently?
  • Create a Table of Contents and an Introduction Page about yourself as a writer

✅ Can Your Child...?
  • Compile a portfolio with 6-8 pieces spanning different genres
  • Write a Reflection Tag for each piece
  • Create a Table of Contents
  • Write an Introduction Page about yourself as a writer
💡 Easier: Select 4 pieces with dictated or fill-in-the-blank reflection tags.
💡 Harder: Write a full "Writer's Memoir" introduction (1 page) about your writing journey with specific examples.
