Main Idea & Supporting Details

October Week 2

Age 9

Informational Text
🎯 Learning Goal

Determine the main idea of an informational text and identify key details that support it.

☀️ Warm-Up: Mystery Bag

Imagine pulling 5 related items from a bag: toothbrush, floss, toothpaste, mouthwash, dental picks. What is the "main idea"? (Dental care!) Each item is a "supporting detail."

📝 Activity: The Main Idea Hand

Read a 2-page informational article. Teach the Main Idea Hand: the palm is the main idea (what the whole text is mostly about), and each finger is a supporting detail (a fact that proves the main idea). Remember: the main idea is not just the topic ("sharks") but what the author is saying about it ("Sharks have unique adaptations that help them survive").

  • Explain the difference between a topic and a main idea
