Publishing & Sharing Writing

May Week 4

Age 9

Creative Writing
🎯 Learning Goal

Publish a final polished piece of writing in a creative format and share it with an audience, experiencing the full writer's journey from idea to publication.

☀️ Warm-Up: Gallery Walk Preview

Look at all your writing pieces from this month. Which shows the most growth? Which are you most proud of? That's the one you'll publish!

📝 Activity: Publishing Party

Choose your best piece and publish it! Options:

  1. Handwritten book with decorated cover, title page, and "About the Author"
  2. Typed and printed with a designed cover
  3. Audio recording with expression and sound effects
  4. Digital book with illustrations on each page

Must include: title, dedication page, the story, and an "About the Author" page with a self-portrait and 3 fun facts.

✍️ Writing Space
✅ Can Your Child...?
  • Publish a clean, formatted, illustrated piece of writing
  • Include title, dedication, story, and About the Author
  • Read aloud with expression to an audience
  • Answer audience questions about your writing
💡 Easier: Publish a 3-paragraph story with a hand-drawn cover.
💡 Harder: Create a digital book with illustrations and read it as a "book launch" event with refreshments.
