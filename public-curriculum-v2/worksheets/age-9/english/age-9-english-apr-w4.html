Dictionary & Thesaurus Skills

April Week 4

Age 9

Vocabulary & Word Study
🎯 Learning Goal

Use a dictionary to find word meanings, pronunciations, parts of speech, and example sentences; use a thesaurus to find synonyms and antonyms for stronger word choice.

☀️ Warm-Up: Dictionary Race

Call out a word. Race to find it in a physical dictionary. Time yourself on 5 words and see if you get faster. What do guide words at the top of each page do?

📝 Activity: Dictionary & Thesaurus Workshop

Dictionary Entry Parts: entry word, pronunciation, part of speech, numbered definitions, example sentence, word origin.

Thesaurus: Synonyms (similar meaning) and antonyms (opposite meaning). Rule: Never use a thesaurus word you can't define — look it up in the dictionary first!

Create a Word Wall of upgraded words.

✍️ Writing Space
✅ Can Your Child...?
  • Look up 4 words in the dictionary with pronunciation, part of speech, and definition
  • Upgrade 8 words in a writing piece using a thesaurus
  • Never use a thesaurus word without knowing its definition
  • Create a Synonym Stairs chart for 2 words
💡 Easier: Use a children's dictionary with simpler definitions.
💡 Harder: Compare definitions across two dictionaries and note differences in nuance.
