Numbers 1 to 5

Early Elementary Math · September Week 1

Ages 5–7

Counting & Cardinality
🎯 Learning Goal

Recognize numbers 1–5 and match them to quantities using one-to-one correspondence.

☀️ Warm-Up: "Five Little Ducks"

Sing "Five Little Ducks" together. Hold up fingers for each duck as you count down from 5 to 1.

🧱 Activity: Number Towers

You need: Lego blocks (or similar), index cards with numbers 1–5

  1. Build a tower with 1 block. Place the card 1 next to it.
  2. Build a tower with 2 blocks. Place the card 2 next to it.
  3. Build a tower with 3 blocks. Place the card 3 next to it.
  4. Build a tower with 4 blocks. Place the card 4 next to it.
  5. Build a tower with 5 blocks. Place the card 5 next to it.

Now mix up the cards! Can you match each number card to the right tower again?

✅ Can Your Child...?
  • Count 5 objects without skipping any
  • Count 5 objects without double-counting
  • Match the number "3" to a group of 3 things
  • Point to the number "5" when asked
💡 Easier: Start with only numbers 1, 2, and 3. Don't rush to 5.
💡 Harder: Have your child write the numbers 1–5 in a tray of sand or salt using their finger.
