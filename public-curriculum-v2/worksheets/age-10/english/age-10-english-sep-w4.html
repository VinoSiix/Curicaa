🎯 Learning Goal

Determine the theme of a story by analysing what the characters learn and what the author wants the reader to understand about life.

☀️ Warm-Up: Topic vs Theme

A topic is what the story is about in one word (e.g. "friendship"). A theme is what the author is saying about that topic (e.g. "True friendship means standing by someone even when it is hard").

Read each topic. Write a possible theme statement on the line.
