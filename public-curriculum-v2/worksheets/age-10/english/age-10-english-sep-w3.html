🔗 Activity: Plot Element Matching

Match each plot term to its definition. Write the correct letter in the blank.

TermAnswerDefinition
1. Exposition a. The turning point or most intense moment of the story
2. Rising Action b. The problems start to resolve and things calm down
3. Climax c. The introduction of characters, setting, and situation
4. Falling Action d. Events build tension and lead toward the peak
5. Resolution e. The story wraps up and loose ends are tied
📝 Activity: Story Analysis

Choose a short story or a chapter from your reading book. Describe each plot element below.

Exposition — Who are the main characters? What is the setting?

   

Climax — What was the most exciting or tense moment?

   

Resolution — How was the main problem solved?
