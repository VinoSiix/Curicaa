🎯 Learning Goal

Build independent reading stamina by choosing "just right" books and tracking daily reading habits with a reading log.

☀️ Warm-Up: My Reading Right Now

Answer the questions below honestly about your current reading habits.

1. What book are you reading right now?  

2. How many pages did you read last time you sat down to read?  

3. On a scale of 1–5, how much are you enjoying it?  

4. How long can you read without getting distracted?  

✋ Activity: The 5-Finger Rule

Use the 5-Finger Rule to check if a book is right for you. Read one full page and count the words you don't know.

  • 0–1 fingers: Too easy — you might get bored
  • 2–3 fingers: Just right — a good challenge
  • 4–5 fingers: Too hard — try it again later in the year

Pick a book from your shelf and test it. How many fingers?
