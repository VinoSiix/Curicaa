☀️ Warm-Up

4 × 10 = 40   40 × 10 = 400   400 × 10 = 4,000   4,000 × 10 = 40,000

What do you notice? Each time you multiply by 10, the digits shift one place to the left (a zero is added at the end).

✏️ Multiply by 10, 100, and 1,000

🔢 Divide by 10, 100, and 1,000

🧱 Exponent Notation
9. 10² = 10 × 10 = 100

11. 1,000 = 103
12. 100 = 102
📝 Digit-Shifting Patterns
