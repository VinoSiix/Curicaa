1.Cross-hatchingA single continuous line without lifting the pen
2.StipplingOverlapping sets of parallel lines in different directions
3.ContourCreating value using only small dots
4.Implied lineA line that is suggested but not actually drawn
5.HatchingA series of parallel lines used to create shading
